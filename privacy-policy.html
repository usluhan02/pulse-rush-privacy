Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 27, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Alihan Uslu ("we", "us", or "our") handles information in connection with the Pulse Rush mobile application ("App").

1. Information We Do Not Collect

Pulse Rush does not collect, store, or transmit any personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, or location. We do not require account registration to use the App.

2. Automatically Collected Information

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may automatically collect certain device information including:

  • Device identifiers (Advertising ID)
  • IP address
  • Device type and operating system
  • App usage data for ad targeting purposes

This data is collected and processed by Google LLC. For more information, please review Google's Privacy Policy.

3. Advertising

We use Google AdMob to serve ads within the App. AdMob may use cookies and similar tracking technologies to personalize ads based on your interests. You can opt out of personalized advertising by adjusting your device settings:

  • iOS: Settings → Privacy → Tracking → disable "Allow Apps to Request to Track"
  • Android: Settings → Google → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization

4. Local Data Storage

The App stores game data (such as high scores and settings) locally on your device only. This data is never transmitted to our servers or any third party.

5. Children's Privacy

Pulse Rush is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided personal information, please contact us and we will take steps to delete such information.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect the information stored locally on your device. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure.

7. GDPR — Users in the European Union

If you are located in the European Union, you have certain rights regarding your personal data under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). Since we do not collect personal data directly, these rights primarily apply to data collected by Google AdMob. Please refer to Google's privacy controls to exercise your rights.

8. CCPA — Users in California

If you are a California resident, you have rights under the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA). We do not sell personal information. For data collected by Google AdMob, please refer to Google's privacy policy and opt-out options.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Continued use of the App after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the new Privacy Policy.
